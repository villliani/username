Gmail Auto-Labeller

Built entirely within the n8n automation platform, this system periodically scans for new emails, uses Google's Gemini AI to determine the most appropriate label, and then dynamically applies it. This solution is designed to tackle inbox clutter, save significant time on manual organization, and ensure important communications are categorized accurately and consistently.
